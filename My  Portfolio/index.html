UI/UX

UI/UX stands for User Interface/User Experience, which refers to the design and interaction of a website or application's visual elements, such as buttons, menus, and forms, as well as the overall experience a user has when interacting with it. Effective UI/UX design aims to create a seamless and intuitive experience for the user, making it easy for them to navigate and achieve their goals, while also ensuring that the design is visually appealing and aesthetically pleasing.
